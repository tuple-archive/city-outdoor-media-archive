The Manchester Canvas

This show stopping digital canvas is situated on Rochdale Road, in the heart of Manchester city centre, The Manchester canvas will be the largest, most central roadside screen offering 100 sq meters of digital canvas. Positioned on one of the busiest commuter routes in to the city, the Manchester Canvas benefits from the high dwell time of three lanes of head on one way traffic heading towards the Northern Quarter, Oldham Road, Ancoats, and also the inner ring road, offering approx. 1,082,000 gross route impacts per 14 day campaign
